Survey for Parents of Autistic Children

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a more inclusive and supportive environment for autistic children and their families when visiting entertainment places. By understanding the unique challenges and needs of these families, we aim to advocate for better facilities, trained staff, and sensory-friendly experiences that ensure enjoyable and stress-free outings.

Our target users are parents and guardians of autistic children who face difficulties in public and entertainment settings. Your feedback will help us identify key areas for improvement and drive positive change in the community.
